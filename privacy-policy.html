Nova IPTV Player Privacy Policy

Effective date: 6 June 2026

Nova IPTV Player is a media player application for macOS. The app does not provide, host, sell, or distribute TV channels, movies, series, or other media content. Users must provide their own IPTV provider account, playlist, or stream source.

Information We Collect

Nova IPTV Player is designed to collect as little personal information as possible.

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • IPTV provider/server details that you enter.
  • Playlist, channel, movie, and series metadata loaded from your IPTV provider.
  • App preferences, favorites, playback settings, and trial/licensing state.

For the Mac App Store version, purchases and restore operations are handled by Apple StoreKit. Apple may process purchase-related information according to Apple's own privacy policy. Nova IPTV Player uses StoreKit purchase status only to unlock app functionality.

If you choose to contact support or send a support report, we may receive diagnostic information needed to investigate the issue, such as app version, operating system version, device/platform details, logs, and error information. Support reports should not be sent unless you choose to share them.

Information We Do Not Collect

Nova IPTV Player does not:

  • Sell personal information.
  • Track users across other companies' apps or websites.
  • Include third-party advertising SDKs.
  • Provide analytics for advertising or cross-app tracking.
  • Host or redistribute IPTV content.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to:

  • Load and play IPTV content from sources you provide.
  • Save app settings and playback preferences.
  • Verify trial, purchase, and restore status.
  • Diagnose crashes, errors, or playback problems when you request support.
  • Improve app reliability and compatibility.

IPTV Providers and Third-Party Services

When you add an IPTV account, playlist, or stream URL, the app connects directly to the provider or server you configured. Those providers are independent third parties. Their privacy practices, content rights, logging, and account policies are controlled by them, not by Nova IPTV Player.

You are responsible for using IPTV services and content that you are legally entitled to access.

Data Storage

App settings and IPTV configuration are stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, macOS may remove app data depending on how the app was installed and removed.

Support information is only received if you choose to send it. We keep support information only for as long as reasonably needed to resolve the support request and maintain app reliability.

Security

Nova IPTV Player uses standard encryption technologies for secure communication where supported by your IPTV provider and for app functionality such as purchase verification and support diagnostics. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we use reasonable safeguards appropriate to the app's functionality.

Children's Privacy

Nova IPTV Player is not directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. IPTV content is provided by the user's configured provider, and content availability may vary.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted with a new effective date.
